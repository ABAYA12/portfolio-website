Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me

What is your typical process for working with a new client?

My process typically involves an initial consultation to understand your needs, followed by a proposal outlining scope, timeline, and cost. Once approved, I'll create wireframes or prototypes for your feedback before moving into development. Throughout the project, I maintain regular communication and provide updates on progress.

How long does it take to complete a typical project?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while more complex web applications can take 2-3 months or more. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific requirements.

What is your pricing structure?

I offer both project-based and hourly pricing options. For most clients, I recommend project-based pricing which provides a clear, upfront cost. The exact price depends on project requirements, complexity, and timeline. I'm happy to provide a detailed quote after our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support after the project is completed?

Yes, I offer ongoing support and maintenance packages to ensure your website or application continues to function optimally. These packages can include regular updates, security monitoring, content updates, and technical support. We can discuss the specific support needs for your project.
